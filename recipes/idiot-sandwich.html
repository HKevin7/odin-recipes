Idiot sandwich

Description

WHAT ARE YOU?!

Ingredients

  • 2 sandwich slices
  • An idiot (can be anyone, preferably someone you know to avoid conflict)

Steps

  1. In order to create this Gordon Ramsey inspired dish, you will need a friend or family member that is preparing a meal as we speak
  2. Have 2 slices of bread ready in both of your hands
  3. Pay close attention to the person preparing the meal, and lookout for mistakes they might make
  4. As soon as you notice the slightest hint of a mistake, make sure you have the person's attention and firmly place one of both sandwich slices on each of the person's cheeks
  5. This next step is very important. When having the person's head between the sandwich slices, make sure they understand the situation they are in by asking them 'WHAT ARE YOU?!'. If they haven't lived under a rock for the past decade, they should understand the message
  6. Did the person admit that they are an idiot sandwich? Great! You succesfully created an idiot sandwich and in addition saved yourself of what could have been a disastrous meal!
