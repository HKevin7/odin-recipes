Peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Description

'Ah'

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter (preferably Jiff, cus it's the superior one. Stop crying)
  • Jam (can be any type, depends on your preferance)
  • 2 sandwich slices
  • Please don't add a baby to this list, it'll end just like the image above

Steps

  1. Take the first sandwhich slice. With a butter knife (NOT a sharp knife, we don't want any accidents happenings in addition to the risks of having an open jar of peanut butter) spread a generous amount of peanut butter on the bottom slice
  2. Take the second sandwhich slice and repeat the previous step, but this time spread the jam (again, a generous amount. No need to be greedy.) on the slice
  3. Finished the steps above? Great! You've just created the best snack possible. Yes, it's that easy, enjoy!
